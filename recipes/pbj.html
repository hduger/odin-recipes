Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

The creamiest and most delicios peanutbutter smeared on one slice of bread with the most flavorful jelly smeared on the other slice of bread, and those two slices pressed together. This forms what is known as the best struggle meal of all time.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Your favorite brand of peanutbutter
  • Your favoirite type of jelly

Steps

  1. Smear a respectable layer of peanutbutter on one slice of bread
  2. Smear a respectable but thinner layer of jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Press those two slices of bread together
  4. Begin your feast
