Lasagna

Description

Lasanga is made with flat lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and meat sauce.

You can either make the meat sauce yourself or you can use store bought meat sauce. Either will work fine but if you want to make your own you will need a meat sauce recipe, and you would use that sauce in place of the one in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • meat sauce
  • 1/2 pound of dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 and 1/2 pounds of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put water on stove to boil
  2. Heat up your meat sauce in a sauce pan
  3. Boil and drain the noodles while the sauce is heating
  4. Heat the oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  5. Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
  7. Allow lasagna to cool for 15 minutes
  8. Serve the lasagna
