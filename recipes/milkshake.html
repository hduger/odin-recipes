Milkshake

Description

A rich and creamy desert perfect for a hot summer day. This is made with milk and your favorite flavor of ice cream. You can also add whatever toppings you like. I like whip cream and chocolate sauce.

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Your favoirite flavor of ice cream
  • Whip cream
  • chocolate sauce

Steps

  1. Put 1 cup of your favorite flavor of ice cream into a blender
  2. Put 1/4 to 1/2 cup of whole milk in your blender depending on how thick you want the milkshake to be
  3. Blend for 1 minute or until smooth
  4. Pour contents of blender into tall glass
  5. Top with whip cream and chocolate sauce
